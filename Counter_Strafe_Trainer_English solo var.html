Counter-Strafe.Тренажер

Train instant stopping before shooting

  1. Hold the indicated key (A or D) — Speed is increasing.
  2. The indicator turns green — instantly tap opposite key to reduce speed to zero.
  3. Click LMB exactly at the moment of stopping. The closer to zero, the more accurate the shot.
